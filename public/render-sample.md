## To solve the system of equations:

1. \( y = 4x + 3 \)
2. \( y = 5x - 8 \)

You can find the value of \( x \) by equating these two equations since \( y \) is the same in both:

### Step 1: Equate the equations
\[
4x + 3 = 5x - 8
\]

### Step 2: Solve for \( x \)
\[
3 + 8 = 5x - 4x
\]
\[
11 = x
\]

### Step 3: Solve for \( y \)
Substitute \( x = 11 \) back into either equation. Using the first equation:

\[
y = 4x + 3
\]
\[
y = 4(11) + 3
\]
\[
y = 44 + 3 = 47
\]

### Final Answer
The solution is:
\[
x = 11, \, y = 47
